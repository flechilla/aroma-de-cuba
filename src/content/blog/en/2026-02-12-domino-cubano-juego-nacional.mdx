## How to Play Cuban Dominoes

### Basic Rules (Double Nine, Pairs)

1. **Shuffle:** All 55 tiles are placed face-down and mixed
2. **Deal:** Each player takes 10 tiles â€” this is "la data"
3. **Sleeping tiles:** The remaining 15 are set aside (untouched)
4. **Opening:** Each player draws one sleeping tile; highest value goes first
5. **Play:** Place a tile matching the values at the ends of the chain
6. **Direction:** Turns move counterclockwise
7. **Win ("Pegarse"):** First player to place all tiles wins
8. **Block ("Tranque"):** If no one can play, lowest hand score wins

### Scoring by Pairs

In **"dos para dos"** (two-on-two) mode, both partners' points combine. The first team to reach the agreed score (usually 100 or 200 points) wins the match.
